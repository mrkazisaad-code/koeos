Why Conversion Stays Low

AI failure is not hypothetical. It is already showing up in losses, liability, and stalled conversion.

The Financial Exposure Is Already Real

99% of organizations reported AI-related financial losses. Average annual loss per firm: $4.4M. Not a future risk — a current operating condition.

Hidden Exposure Is Larger Than Reporting Shows

Most firms measure AI at portfolio level. Few measure whether it is reaching governed workflows or commercially defensible operations. The gap accumulates as stranded spend, margin leakage, and undefended outputs.

Operating Models Were Not Built for This Shift

AI reduced the cost of software and cognition. Most operating models were designed for a world where both were scarce. Until the model is redesigned, conversion stays low — regardless of how much capability is deployed.
